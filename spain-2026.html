LA FURIA ROJA THEME

A custom-built, immersive celebration theme designed for blahg-2.0 to honor Spain’s historic triumph at the 2026 FIFA World Cup.

Experience Live on blahg-2.0 ➔

Design & Features

Crafted with precision to combine modern UI architecture with stadium-level football energy.

🎨

La Roja Palette

Vibrant Spanish Crimson (#C60B1E) and Championship Gold (#FFC400) layered over deep velvet dark crimson (#120306).

🏟️

Stadium Pitch Canvas

Replaces standard graph grid squares with an SVG football pitch diagram featuring touchlines, halfway circle, penalty boxes, and turf stripes.

⚽

Precision Football Pointer

A sharp, high-precision arrow cursor tip paired with a mini red and gold soccer ball tail, plus glowing hover interactions.

✨

Subtle Sparkle Trail

A lightweight HTML5 canvas particle trail that emits subtle golden and crimson micro-sparkles as your cursor moves across the screen.

Technical Implementation

Built directly into Next.js 16 App Router using next-themes multi-theme resolution:

  • Configured themes={['light', 'dark', 'spain']} with automatic default activation until July 25, 2026.
  • CSS custom variants @custom-variant spain using Tailwind v4.
  • Zero-dependency canvas confetti and cursor particle effects.
